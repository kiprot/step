[Project]Photos from the AMNH and the Met Museums

I recently started a casual project to compile photos of some artifacts, statues, and figurines at the American Museum of National History, and the Metropolitan Museum. Both are in New York City, and are rich in exquisite representations of a lot of things, from human history, to marine biology. I plan to do this, just to see how much amateur photography I may be willing to do.

As a start, I compiled some photos of the T-Rex that I took using my phone camera. Click the button to see random pictures of the famous dinosaur
